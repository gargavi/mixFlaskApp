How to use?


You need to find the exact song id that you want to match the song to. Just copy this and the app will find the song that you want.

The reason for this is that there are many artist with the same name Songs and even artists who have the same song name in various formats so this is the easiest way to be clear.

This is where you select parameters.

  1. The Exact Key will find songs that are the exact key if checked or will find songs that are one away according to the Camelot Wheel.
  2. The Double Tempo will include songs that are twice as fast
  3. The Half Tempo will include songs that are half as fast
  4. The Normal Tempo will include songs that are as fast
  5. The Popularity will sort the resulting songs with the most popular songs showing up first.
  6. The Tightness will say how close the tempo has to be: a one means that it has be within 3 beats of the given song and a 10 means it can be within 30 beats. This number has be within 1 and 10. (A zero will give you nothing)
  7. The Number just determines how many songs will be displayed at once.
